Summer borscht

Description

I always remember going to my grandparents house, and when dinner time came my grandma went into the pantry and came back with a jar of soup. She would heat it up filling the house with a strong dill and sorrel smell that's become very nostalgic for me. We would then sit down and eat bowls of hot summer borscht cooled down slightly with a spoon of sour cream, as well as crusty buns she had made with butter spread all over.
