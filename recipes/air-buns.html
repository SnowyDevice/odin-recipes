Air Buns

Description

These buns are light, airy, have a wonderful crisp crust formed, and are perfect for small sandwiches or dipping in soup.

Ingredients

  • 1pkg yeast
  • 1tsp. sugar
  • 1/2c. warm water
  • 9c. flour
  • 2tsp. salt
  • 1c. lard or oil
  • 1/2c. sugar
  • 1tbsp. vinegar
  • 4c. water

Steps

  1. Soak yeast and sugar in the warm water for 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl add flour and make a well in it then add remaining ingredients incuding yeast mixture.
  3. Stir with a spoon until dough forms a mass that leaves the side of the bowl.
  4. Knead dough by hand until smooth and elastic, a rather stiff dough, adding a little more flour if necessary.
  5. Set aside to rise in a warm place until almost double in size. Then punch down and repeat once or twice more.
  6. Form dough into buns the size of an egg, set on pans, cover and let rise until double in size.
  7. Bake in 400Â° oven for 25 minutes.
